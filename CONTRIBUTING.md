# Contributing

Thanks for helping build a useful, neutral resource on AI agent governance.

## What belongs here

Frameworks, tools, standards, and writing that help answer: **what is an AI agent allowed to do, who approves consequential actions, and how is that recorded?**

## Guidelines

- **One line per entry.** Name, link, and a single sentence on what it is and why it's relevant.
- **Most specific category wins.** A tool that does both policy *and* receipts goes where its primary strength is.
- **Disclose affiliation.** If you maintain or work on the project, label it *(maintainer project)* or say so in the PR. We welcome maintainer entries — we just label them.
- **No marketing voice.** Factual and technical. Entries that read like ad copy will be edited or declined.
- **Working links only.** Link to the project/docs, not a paywalled landing page where possible.

## How to submit

1. Fork and add your entry to the appropriate section of `README.md`, keeping alphabetical-ish order within reason.
2. Open a PR with a short description of the entry's governance relevance.
3. A maintainer will review for fit and tone.
